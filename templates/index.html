About Me did it work

Hello! I'm Dauhson (pronounced Dawson) Capps, a passionate software engineer with a specialization in AI and ML. Hailing from a small town in Utah, my roots are firmly planted in the hands-on world of mechanics. Growing up, my family owned a wrecking yard where I spent countless hours tinkering and understanding the intricacies of machines. This love for mechanics naturally evolved into a fascination with the digital realm, and I became obsessed with coding and technology. Beyond the screen, I'm a dedicated father of two, an avid drone flyer, and a 3D printing enthusiast. My academic journey led me to achieve the Dean's list, a testament to my unwavering commitment to excellence. Two quotes that guide my ethos are: "We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them" and "Strive not to be a success, but rather to be of value," both by Albert Einstein. In all aspects of life, whether it's in front of a computer or under the hood of a car, I believe in adding value and pushing the boundaries of conventional thinking.
